Questions about the box
Good to know
What does it taste like?+
Ibérico is a truly remarkable experience because it doesn't quite taste like pork. The natural umami, tenderness, and superior marbling leave unsuspecting guests wondering if they're eating pork or the finest Wagyu or Kobe steak.
Is it humanely raised & free of hormones?+
Yes. Our Ibérico is free-range and slow-grown on family-owned farms, with no added hormones and no antibiotics — the way this heirloom breed has been raised for generations.
How should I cook it?+
Keep it simple — salt, pepper, and a hot pan, cooked to medium-rare to let the marbling shine. Every box includes our recipe guide with ideas for each cut.
Why "The Wagyu of Pork™"?+
Like Wagyu beef, Ibérico is prized for its intense marbling and melt-in-your-mouth texture — the result of an heirloom breed raised slowly on a natural diet. Many diners genuinely can't tell it apart from a fine steak.
