How it works

The World Health Organization's oral rehydration formula needs three things: sodium, glucose, and water. We adapted it with real fruit juice and real cane sugar.

  • 1Pour into 8–16oz cold water — over ice is ideal
  • 2Stir for 10 seconds. Real fruit juice powder dissolves fast, no chalky residue
  • 3Absorbs 3x faster than water — sodium-glucose cotransport pulls water into your bloodstream
