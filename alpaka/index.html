The details

Description

Stow your cards and cash quickly in a compact pouch. Designed for those who want to carry as little weight as possible, the Zip Pouch Pro has secure storage space for frequently used items such as credit cards and bank notes. With an extra pocket for coins or smaller items like your spare SIM card, you'll find room even for business cards too!

As a bonus, it comes with three external slots for additional space to carry a small torch, lighter, etc.

Materials
  • Exterior: X-Pac® VX21 (Black, Dark Green, Slate Gray), X-Pac® VX25 (Hot Orange)
  • 100% carbon-neutral fabric
  • Interior: 210D Orange Ripstop Nylon lining (Black, Dark Green, Slate Gray); 200D Gray X-grid Ripstop Nylon (Orange)
Dimensions
  • Height: 8.5 cm (3.34")
  • Width: 13 cm (5.11")
  • Weight: 40 g
