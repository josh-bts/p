Because gut health impacts more than just digestion

Chronic digestive upset is one of the most common signs of a food allergy or sensitivity in dogs. But poor gut health means more than just gross poops and room-clearing gas.

The gut is where nutrients are absorbed, the immune system is regulated, inflammation begins (or calms), skin health is influenced, and energy is supported. So when your dog's gut health is out of balance, symptoms show up everywhere — loose stool, itchy skin, ear infections, low energy, and more. This food is designed to help rebalance your dog's gut, one bite at a time.

Cricket protein is naturally rich in essential amino acids, B-vitamins, healthy fats, and antioxidants that support your dog's overall health and vitality.
Naturally rich in chitin, a prebiotic fiber, to help nourish good gut bacteria and support healthy digestion.
Powered by plant-based DHA from marine microalgae oil to help support brain health and everyday mental sharpness.
Crafted without common allergens such as chicken, beef, fish, wheat, soy, and corn to help minimize food-related sensitivities.
Cricket protein uses far less land, water, and greenhouse gases than traditional meat, making it better for your dog and the planet.
