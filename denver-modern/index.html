The Vail Bar & Counter Stool is a beautifully crafted piece that blends modern design with classic craftsmanship. It features a solid wood dowel, channel-tufted upholstery, and leather strap detailing. With a powder-coated steel frame and high-density foam padding, it's as comfortable as it is elegant — available in two heights and a wide range of finishes.
Counter: 24" seat height  ·  Bar: 30" seat height. Measure to the bottom of your counter or bar overhang to choose: standard counter is ~35"–36", standard bar is ~42".
Boucle & fabric: Blot spills immediately with a clean, colorfast towel; spot clean with a damp cloth and air dry. Our boucle is treated with DEFEND for maximum stain resistance.

Leather: Keep out of direct sunlight and away from heat. Rub out minor scratches lightly with a finger; wipe oil-based spots with a dry cloth.
How do I know which height I need — bar or counter?
Measure to the bottom of your counter or bar overhang. For standard counter height, measure ~35"–36" (choose Counter, 24" seat); for bar height ~42" (choose Bar, 30" seat).
