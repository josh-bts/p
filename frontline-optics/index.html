Impact-Resistant Sunglasses Built for Tough Jobs

The Pomona sunglasses are engineered to withstand the rigors of your demanding job, offering the perfect balance of comfort, durability, and style. Constructed from high-strength polycarbonate, these sunglasses are designed to stay securely in place, even during the most intense situations. Paired with black polycarbonate polarized lenses, they deliver a clean, professional look whether you're on or off duty. The Pomona meets ANSI Z.87 impact requirements and satisfies OSHA specifications for jobs that do not require side impact or chemical splash resistance.

True-To-Life Vision with FLOVEX™ Lens Tech

Instead of dulling or enhancing colors, FLOVEX™ maintains a natural, true-to-life visual experience while drastically reducing glare. The result is clean, comfortable optics you can wear all day — so natural, you may forget you're wearing them.
