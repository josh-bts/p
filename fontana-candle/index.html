Calm in a jar

Lavender is known for its calming and balancing properties with a clean, pure aroma. This certified non-toxic candle is made from 100% natural lavender essential oils, steam-distilled for the highest quality — ideal for winding down, meditation, or simply filling your home with a gentle, refreshing scent.

If you love lavender but want a bolder floral throw, try our Wildflower Candle.

Ingredients
Beeswax (Apis Mellifera) · Coconut Oil (Cocos Nucifera) · Lavender Oil (Lavandula Angustifolia) · Untreated wooden wick.
MADE SAFE® certified — free of any and all toxins known or suspected to harm human health, animals, or ecosystems.

The first burn matters: let the melted wax reach the edge of the jar — about 2–3+ hours — to set the foundation for an even, optimal burn every time after.

Essential-oil candles carry a lighter, more natural scent than synthetic fragrance candles — perfect for those with sensitivities, and for anyone who wants traditional candle ambiance without the toxins. Trim the wooden wick and keep within sight while burning.
