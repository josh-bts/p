Will my facial hair grow back thicker or darker?+
No. It's a myth that removing hair from your face makes it grow back differently. It's scientifically proven that your peach fuzz (vellus hair) will NOT grow back thicker or darker after dermaplaning. LUXE+ removes hair from your skin's surface and does not affect the hair follicle in any way.

For best results, use DERMAFLASH LUXE+ once a week. Weekly use maintains smooth, fuzz-free skin and is clinically proven to improve the look of fine lines, wrinkles, and dark spots over time.
