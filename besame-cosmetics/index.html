One of the first colors our founder discovered on her mission to revive iconic cosmetic styles for modern women, Bésame Red — 1920 is the cornerstone of our line of bold and beautiful lipstick shades. This true red with a cool-blue undertone compliments a wide range of complexions with a semi-matte look that stays perfectly in place from coffee shops to cocktail hours and everything in between. Enriched with squalane and Vitamin E, it feels great on the lips while delivering the unabashed confidence of the daring "New Woman" of the 1920s.
Our application guide follows a two-step process created in the 1940s by movie-industry make-up artists to give actresses perfect, camera-ready lips that last through a long day of filming.
  1. Prepare lips: Ensure lips are clean and hydrated, free of dryness.
  2. Outline & fill: Define the lip line, then fill with colour and blot for long, even wear.
